Faction

The Undertow

A powerful smuggling syndicate rooted in the city’s drowned docks, warehouses, canals, and forgotten coastal districts. Most members are desperate humans chasing a payout; their leader, Marrowdeep, is something other than human wearing a flawless disguise. They work quietly: moving contraband, tightening invisible nets, and waiting for the city to sink one deal at a time.

Rumors & Myths

  • Some say the Undertow’s leader breathes seawater and has a second heartbeat like a wave under stone.
  • Dockhands whisper that three captains drowned and stood back up, now answering to the deep.
  • Smugglers swear there’s a tattoo-map that marks an airless vault beneath the harbor.
  • Old sailors insist shadows ripple like water around the Undertow’s safehouses.

Lore (Public knowledge)

“The tide pulls all things under.” The Undertow survives on human grit, cold efficiency, and smuggling mastery. What keeps them truly feared is the unseen presence of Marrowdeep, an eldritch titan in human form whose patience stretches for years. They favor quiet intimidation over open violence and let debt, secrecy, and contraband do the drowning.

What they do

  • Smuggle weapons, shard-infused items, mutagenic tech, and illegal freight.
  • Run black-market trades tied to mutation and eldritch artifacts.
  • Infiltrate and corrupt from the inside, slowly.

Why they do it

  • Marrowdeep believes every city eventually sinks.
  • They collect leverage instead of territory.
  • They see mutation and the sea as inevitable forces.

GM Section: Leadership

Marrowdeep — Titan in Human Form

Born as Ellis Crane, reshaped by something ancient beneath the waves. Wears a human shape; leaks abyssal traits: pitch eyes, tidewater breath, a second heartbeat, shadows moving as if underwater. Seeks inevitability, not conquest.

Stats

  • Reflex 3 | Strength 6 | Social 5 | Smarts 4 | Durability 7 | Armor 3

Powers

  • Lvl 1 — Abyssal Breath: breathe air/water; unsettling undertones.
  • Lvl 2 — Bent Pressure: local pressure control; bursts/knockback.
  • Lvl 3 — Partial Unmasking: tentacles, crushing strength, fear aura.
  • Lvl 4 (2 PP) — Abyssal Manifestation: kaiju surge, shadow limbs, psychic drowning.

GM Section: Operations

Methods

  • Smuggling: weapons, shard items, mutagenic tech, illegal freight.
  • Extortion: quiet intimidation, rare open violence.
  • Black Market Trade: mutation/eldritch/unstable tech focus.
  • Infiltration: insider corruption and long-term manipulation.

Goals & Philosophy

  • Every system breaks; every city sinks.
  • Every person is one tragedy away from drowning.
  • Collect leverage over outright conquest.

GM Section: Encounters & Hooks

Player Interactions

  • Goons: street fights, ambushes, smuggling busts.
  • Sinkers: tougher fights; eerie loyalists with minor mutations.
  • The Drowned: mini-bosses in water-themed arenas.
  • Marrowdeep: late-campaign reveal; escalating stages.

Adventure Hooks

  • Missing Shipment: shard artifacts vanish into Undertow hands.
  • Floating Bodies: survivors suddenly breathe water at the docks.
  • Captain’s Wake: a Drowned abducts a scientist into tunnels.
  • Smuggler’s Map: tattooed skin hides a map beneath the city.

GM Section: Aesthetic & Tone

Visual Themes

  • Barnacle textures, ropes, nets, lantern-lit docks.
  • Salt-soaked clothing; water pooled where none should be.
  • Dripping shadows; quiet wave sounds indoors.

Summary Vibe

  • Crime noir meets cosmic horror.
  • Organized smuggling plus mutant transformations.
  • Slow inevitability: the city sinks piece by piece.
