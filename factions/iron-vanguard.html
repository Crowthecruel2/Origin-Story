Faction

Iron Vanguard

A mercenary hero company forged in war. They wear exosuits, sign contracts, and sanitize collateral damage through PR. To clients they are polished heroes; up close they are a paid army.

Rumors & Reports

  • The Bastion base is half fortress, half branding studio.
  • They only fight the Undertow or mutants when a contract clears.
  • Collateral damage is written off as "necessary expenditure."
  • Unauthorized possession of Vanguard tech is a federal crime.

Lore (Public knowledge)

The Vanguard motto to the public is "We protect because we can." Internally it is "Strength, Order, Victory." Profit, reputation, and tactical advantage come before civilians. They claim heroism; they operate like soldiers.

How they operate

  • Contracts dictate deployment and force.
  • Captains act as celebrity commanders.
  • PR sanitizes every mission report.

Why they respond

  • Profitable contracts or optics.
  • Testing new tech in live scenarios.
  • Securing market share over Aegis.

GM Section: Structure & Tech

Command tiers

  • Command: executives and decorated veterans.
  • Captains: elite exosuit pilots leading squads.
  • Vanguards: licensed armored operatives.
  • Recruits: trainees earning their plates.
  • Support Corps: engineers, medics, PR, drone ops.

Gear highlights

  • Exosuits, hazard mechs, modular jetpacks.
  • Smart rifles, shield projectors, anti-mutant tools.
  • Rumored shard-replicated alloys and stolen villain tech.
