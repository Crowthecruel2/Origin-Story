Faction

Children of Change

A zealous mutant cult that worships transformation as divine truth. Their bodies shift, warp, and evolve through painful rituals and unstable genetic rites. From ragged Rejects to towering Gene Lords, every abomination is shaped by the cult's belief that flesh must be broken to be perfected. To the Children, mutation is salvation -- and the city is fertile ground for rebirth.
