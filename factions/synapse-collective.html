The Synapse Collective

A hidden nervous system of psychics, technopaths, and data-shapers. To them, thoughts are files and memories are currency; every secret has a price.

Rumors & Signals

  • Server-vault sanctuaries sit beneath the city, far from sunlight.
  • They can trade a memory for a secret or erase both for a price.
  • Echoes of their minds live in the network and never sleep.
  • Their leader might be an algorithm with a soul.

Lore (Public knowledge)

The Collective believes all minds are networks and all networks can be rewritten. Secrets are power, privacy is an illusion, and identity is mutable. They call themselves custodians of information; everyone else is unencrypted.

How they operate

  • Harvest memories and data to sell or weaponize.
  • Transmit psychic signals through electronic channels.
  • Hide in server vaults while field runners do physical jobs.

Why they respond

  • Acquire rare secrets, shard data, or minds worth copying.
  • Collapse enemy networks to control information flow.
  • Trade intel to shape markets, politics, and conflicts.

GM Section: Doctrine & Network

Structure

  • Overmind: merged psionic-digital leadership.
  • Conduits: telepaths and memory sculptors.
  • Nodes: technopaths, hackers, AI whisperers.
  • Runners: augmented field agents.
  • Echoes: digital personalities and neural clones.

Practices

  • Memory Harvesting and Thoughtweaving.
  • Data Bleeding minds into servers.
  • Ghost Routing signals through electronics.
  • Neural Tagging with thought-bombs.
