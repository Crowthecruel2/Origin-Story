## Description
Add a short description for Water Techie.

_Only this player can use the supertech unless specified otherwise._

---

Hydro Gloves: A pair of rubberized gloves with micro-pumps and moisture collectors.  
**Cost:** $10  
**Effect:** Lets you extract water from the air or liquids. Produces up to **1 liter per turn**. Reduces taken water/cold damage by 1.  
**Requirements:** None

---
Slickfoam Capsule: A capsule filled with expanding slick-foam.  
**Cost:** $10  
**Effect:** Creates a **5m patch** of hyper-slippery foam.

- Anyone moving through must roll Reflex 5 or fall prone.
    
- Foam lasts 5 turns.  
    **Requirements:** None
    

---

Chillburst Vial: A vial of fast-evaporating cryo-fluid.  
**Cost:** $50  
**Effect:** On breaking, creates a **5m burst of freezing mist**.

- Deals **1d4 cold damage**.
    
- Reflex 5 or movement is halved for 1 turn.  
    **Requirements:** None
    

---

Basic Hydromechanics Bench: A reinforced metal bench with pumps, pressure lines, freeze chambers, and filters.  
**Cost:** $100  
**Requirements:** None

---

Cryo Slush Grenade: A metal sphere filled with rapidly expanding cryo-slush.  
**Cost:** $100  
**Effect:** On detonation creates a **5m frost patch**.

- Immediate **1d6 cold damage**
    
- Targets must pass a Durability 5 check or take **1d4 cold damage for 3 turns**  
    **Requirements:** Basic Hydromechanics Bench
    

---

Pressure Nozzle: A high-pressure sprayer attachment for projecting chemicals.  
**Cost:** $1000  
**Effect:** Increases range of your liquids and chemicals to **20m**.  
**Requirements:** Basic Hydromechanics Bench

---

Insulated Diving Suit: A heavy-duty diving suit with internal pressure equalization.  
**Cost:** $1000  
**Effect:**

- Reduces taken **cold and water damage by 4**
    
- Allows underwater breathing for **1 hour**  
    **Requirements:** None
    

---

Water Lance Charge: A hydraulic explosive that releases a compressed jet of cutting water.  
**Cost:** $1000  
**Effect:** Creates a **10m linear water blast**.

- Deals **2d6 cutting damage**
    
- Ignores **2 armor**
    
- Pierces through soft cover  
    **Requirements:** Basic Hydromechanics Bench
