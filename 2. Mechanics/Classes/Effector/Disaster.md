## Description
Indiscriminate firepower.

| LEVEL | POINTS | ABILITY                                                                                                                                                                                             |
| ----- | ------ | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1     | 1      | **Seismic**<br>-The land around you shakes causing enemies to fall prone and take 1d4 damage. They fall on a failed reflex check of 6. This affect happens to everyone but you in a 3 block radius. |
| 1     | 1      | **Meteor**<br>-Summon a asteroid that falls on an enemy. The meteor does 1d20 damage to everything in a 20m radius.                                                                                 |
