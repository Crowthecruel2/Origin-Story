This ability allows the player to build mechsuits. Only this player can use the supertech unless specified otherwise.

  

  ****
Lv1
---


**Bodysuit**: A skintight bodysuit that doesn't provide any protection. Makes you look slightly cooler. Necessary to use any of the mechs.

Cost: $10

Requirements: none[br]‌

---


**Big Pistol**: A Comically large pistol that can be stored inside mechs as a emergency sidearm.

Cost: $10

Range: 10m

Damage: 1D10

Requirements: None[br]‌

---


**Mech Rifle**: A massive gun that is made to be used by the mechs. The gun can still be used by people but only as a 2 handed weapon.

Cost: $100

Damage: nD10 n = to the size of the mech that is being used. n = 1 is no mech.

Range: 100m

Requirements: None[br]‌

---


**Ram**: A melee weapon that can take a beating. Mostly made of cheap materials like concrete and metal.

Cost: $100

Damage: 1D6 + Strength

Range: Touch

Requirements: None[br]‌

---


**Mech Workbench**: A bench with tools to build cheap mechsuits and better weapons. Needs 1 hour to pack or unpack.

Cost: $100

Requirements: None[br]‌

---


**Basic Mechsuit**: A small mechsuit that seats 1 person and still fits through normal doors. Basically just there to help build bigger mechs or carry heavy objects/weapons.

Cost: $100

HP: 50

Movespeed: 15m per turn

Armor: 1

Weapon slots: 2

Effects: Doubles production speed for bigger mechs. (Doesn't stack) Can carry Mech weapons one handed. Increases strength to 10. There is a 1/4 chance that the enemy hits the player instead of the mech.

Requirements: Mech Workbench.[br]‌

---


**Flamethrower**: A barrel to launch liquid pain at your enemies with.

Cost: $500

Range:10m

Damage: 1d4 in a 10m cone.

Effect: Causes burning. Burning deals 1 damage per turn.

Requirements: Mech Workbench[br]‌

---


**Shield**: A massive Metal slab welded to the arm of a mech.

Cost: $500

Weapon slots: 1

Effect: +1 armor to your mech.

Requirements: Mech Workbench[br]‌

---


**Rotary Concrete Foam Launcher**: A grenade launcher that fires Concrete foam grenades.

Cost: $1000

Range: 50m

Effect: Launches Concrete foam grenades.

Requirements: Fortification Tinker Lv 1[br]‌

---


**Mech Sprayer**: A massive hose like sprayer built into a mech.

Cost: $1000

Effect: Uses a Weapon slot. Allows the mech to contain and spray chemicals safely.

Requirements: Chemical Tinker Lv 1 

---

**Mulcher Mod**: A grinding mouth device that grinds organic waste into bio slurry.

Cost: $1000

Effect: Turns organic waste into random bio-samples

Requirements: Bio Horror Techie Lv 1
