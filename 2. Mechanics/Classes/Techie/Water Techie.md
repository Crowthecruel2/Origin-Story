
Only this player can use the supertech unless specified otherwise.


# Lv1

Ignition Gloves: A pair of fire proof gloves with a lighter built in.
Cost: $10
Effects: Can ignite materials with touch. Reduces taken fire damage by 1.
Requirements: None


---
Firestarter Paste: A thick paste made of flammable materials that burn for a long time.
Cost: $10
Effects: Burns for 10 turns doing 1 damage per turn. Waterproof.
Requirements: None

---
Flashfuel Bottle: A small capsule filled with a volatile liquid.
Cost: $50
Effects: Creates a 5m radius blast of light and fire. Deals 1d4 damage. Targets must make a reflex check of 5 or be blinded for 1 turn.
Requirements: None

---
Basic Pyrotechnics Bench: A metal bench with ignition chambers, pressurized tanks, and controlled flame nozzles.
Cost: $100
Requirements: None

---
Incendiary Gel: A sticky gel that burns brightly
Cost: $100
Effects: Does 1d6 damage when thrown. Burns for 3 turns doing 1d4 damage a turn. Waterproof
Requirements: Basic Pyrotechnics Bench

---
Flame Nozzle: A weak flamethrower that can launch your chemicals further.
Cost: $1000
Effect: Increases the effective range of your chemicals to 20m.
Requirements: Basic Pyrotechnics Bench

---
Flame Suit: A insulated suit filled with fireproof materials.
Cost: $1000
Effect: Reduces taken fire damage by 4.

---
Thermite Bomb: A large bundle of vials and packages with wires coming out of it.
Cost: $1000
Effect: Causes an 10m explosion that coats everything in range with thermite. Coated enemies take 1d10 fire damage per turn for 10 turns.
