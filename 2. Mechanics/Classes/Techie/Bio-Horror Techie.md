
Only this player can use the supertech unless specified otherwise.

# Lv1

Gene Synthesizer: A machine that spins junk DNA into the DNA of your choice.  
Cost: $100  
Requirements: None

---


Gene Implanter: A injection device.  
Cost: $100  
Effect: Allows you to install a gene mod once per turn. A human can only have 5 gene mods at one time or penalties start to happen.  
Requirements: None

---
Portable Gene Scanner: A wand-style device that identifies biological material.
Cost: $50
Effect: Reveals what species' DNA is present in any sample and whether it’s viable for synthesis.
Requirements: None


---


Cat Mod: A gene mod made of cat DNA.  
Cost: 1kg of Cat flesh or blood.  
Effect: After Implantation it allows the player to see in the dark and makes the player immune to fall damage.  
Requirements: Gene Synthesizer

---


Bat Mod: A gene mod made of bat DNA.  
Cost: 1kg of Bat flesh or blood.  
Effect: After Implantation it allows the player to see in a 20m radius with blindsight.  
Requirements: Gene Synthesizer

---


Bird Mod: A gene mod made of bird DNA  
Cost: 1kg of bird flesh or blood.  
Effect: After Implantation it allows the player to grow wings and fly.  
Requirements: Gene Synthesizer

---


Crab mod: A gene mod made of Crab DNA  
Cost: 1kg of crab flesh or blood.  
Effect: After Implantation it forces the player to grow a crab claw which gives you a 1d8 melee dice and you grow armor plates that give you 1 armor.  
Requirements: Gene Synthesizer

---


Lobster mod: A gene mod made of lobster DNA  
Cost: 1kg of lobster flesh or blood.  
Effect: After Implantation the player stops aging and instead the player grows larger every year.  
Requirements: Gene Synthesizer

---


Spider mod: A gene mod made of spider DNA  
Cost: 1kg of spider flesh or ichor.  
Effect: After Implantation the player can climb wall and his bite deals 3d4 poison damage after 3 turns.  
Requirements: Gene Synthesizer

---

Lizard mod: A gene mod made of general reptile DNA.
Cost: 1kg of reptile flesh or blood.
Effect: After Implantation the player becomes invisible to infrared and starts regenerating 1 Hp per turn.
Requirements: Gene Synthesizer

---
Rat Mod: A gene mod made of rodent DNA

Cost: 1kg rat flesh or blood
Effect: Advantage on stealth; immune to disease; squeeze through tiny holes
Requirements: Gene Synthesizer
