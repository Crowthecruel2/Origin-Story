## Description
The powers of your average action movie protagonist

| LEVEL | POINTS | ABILITY                                                                               |
| ----- | ------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1     | 1      | **Hero's Limits**<br>-Every 3rd shot with a gun doesn't take ammo                     |
| 1     | 1      | **Scene Maker**<br>-You can kick down doors with advantage                            |
| 1     | 1      | **Last Stand**<br>You survive for 1 more turn after dying                             |
| 2     | 1      | **Winner's Stand**<br>You can extend your time before dying by 1 if you kill an enemy |
