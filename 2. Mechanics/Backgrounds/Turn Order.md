Players draw a card from the initiative Deck.

The character or units on the card activate

# Start Phase:
Abilities that say at start of turn activate

# Movement Phase
Units may move up to their move value in centimeters

Keywords: Fly, Momentum, Swim, Immobile, Stunned

# Attack Phase
Units may declare an attack for every unit in the model. Units can not declare ranged attacks into a melee fight, unless they are precise or ruthless.

# End Phase
Abilities that say at end of turn activate
