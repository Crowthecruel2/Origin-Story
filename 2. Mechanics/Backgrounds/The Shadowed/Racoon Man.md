Lore: Dark Brooding Protector of Brighton. Often Followed by his Bird themed sidekicks. One of the last people you want to meet after littering in a alleyway. His justice is absolute and brutal. Not Actually a racoon. Not sure if he is a man. Trained by the Nipalesian Warrior God Ninja Monks into a perfect weapon of justice.
