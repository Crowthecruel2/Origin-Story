Lore: The lord of the Sequenced. A order of religious nutjobs that think that the next step for the human race is to incorporate animal DNA into themselves to ascend. He appears as a hulking hunched over Lizard Rat Man Hybrid in a lab coat. He seeks not only the upliftment of humanity but animals as well. His greatest creation is his loyal henchman Igoreous. A rat which he augmented with Human DNA and a mutagen of his own creation.
