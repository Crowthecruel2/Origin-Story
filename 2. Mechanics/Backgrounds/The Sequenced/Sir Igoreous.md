Lore: A rat given human form by his lord [[Doctor Devious Nefarious Evil]]. He is a human rat hybrid of abnormal size. Towering over even Doctor Devious himself. He may not be the smartest monster in his lords army but he is by far one of the strongest. His DNA seems to shift and adapt while he is under stress.
